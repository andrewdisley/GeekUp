* We're looking for volunteers for this month's pre-arranged talks, so if you're interested, please do get in touch *

This month there'll be three 20:20 style presentations, two of which are pre-arranged, with the third slot left open to all comers. So if you think you have something cool to talk about that you'd like to share, then prepare it as a 20:20 style presentation, and we'll leave it open to the audiencemob to decide on the night who gets to talk. Rob has outlined the 20:20 format on the GeekUp wiki but it's essentially 20 slides, with 20 seconds per slide.

So far for scheduled talks we have: Phil Winstanley, who will be giving a MIX Mastication - a quick tour of what was announced @ MIX, what it means, and why you should care, even if you hate Microsoft.
