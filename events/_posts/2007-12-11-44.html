GeekUp Manchester takes place on the second Tuesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This month we have 2 confirmed talks and a potential 3rd talk, we've reserved the Boardroom which is upstairs.

A quick introduction to Virtual Private Networks by Jon Spriggs and Using Wordpress as a light CMS by Simon Wheatley
