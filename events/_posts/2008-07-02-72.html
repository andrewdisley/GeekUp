GeekUp Sheffield takes place on the first Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This month Sheffield GeekUp's lineup includes the following highlights to mark the end of our short tenure at the Fat Cat:

Huddle facilitated by Jag Gill - RTFM: Using Metrics to Encourage Agility

Lightning Talks by:
- Paul Brabban: "Idiot's Guide to Getting Your First Cert"
- James Boardwell: "internet sensibilities: remodelling humans"

KeyNote
- Rob Lee of Rattle Research on Hardware Hacking.

Question Time:
- Our panel of guest contributors wrap up by offering their solutions, ideas and opinions to the current hot topics stirring geekdom. Bring out your issues and see what they have to say - add your question to the comments below.
