GeekUp Leeds takes place on the third Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

We thought we'd have a nice chilled out GeekUp this month in the name of BarCamp rehab! To help us relax, we going to have a bit of a wii night, so bring your wii sticks along if you fancy a game!

If anyone fancies bringing their wii, we're borrowing a second projector.
