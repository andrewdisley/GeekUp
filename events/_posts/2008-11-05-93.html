GeekUp Sheffield takes place on the first Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

We've surrendered our usual venue this month for the Sheffield International Documentary Festival. Instead of huddles and talks, we'll be doing our own screening of a series of short geeky films and animations as a wind-down before the enormity of BarCamp Sheffield at the end of November.

If you have any short geeky films that you've made or think are worthy of the GeekUp Film Festival, then message me or add a comment below.
