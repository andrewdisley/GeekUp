GeekUp Sheffield takes place on the first Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This month is the first anniversary of GeekUp in Sheffield. Come and help us celebrate. There'll be plenty of fun and games as well as a talk or two.

We'll also be building the programme for the next year and getting the ball rolling for BashMash 2 (21 May) and BarCamp Sheffield 3 (19-21 June).
