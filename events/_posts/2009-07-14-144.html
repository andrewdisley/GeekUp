GeekUp Manchester takes place on the second Tuesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

NOTE: Venue Change and free pre-registration required, BBQ food option (£5 fee)

This month GeekUp Manchester will not be at the usual venue, instead we have joined forces with Manchester Digital and Northern Digitals to bring you the Manchester Digital Summer BBQ.

Like all GeekUp's the event is FREE but we ask that you pre-register so the venue has an idea of numbers. You can if you want to optionally during registration pay £5 for the BBQ food that will be available during the event.

Manchester events are purely social, come along and have good chinwag.
