GeekUp Leeds takes place on the third Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This month is being organised by Tim "Chippy" Waters

Talks:

  • George Marselis talking in depth about Social Responsibility of Geeks.
  • Darren Cronian from My Life in Leeds and talking about his experiences and issues blogging and the reception of his new project.
  • Pirate Party UK presentation by party member & regional meet-up
  • Rahoul Baruah (baz) techietubbies 20:20

We'll also have a few dedicated discussion tables for after the talks. Topics so far may include: Pirate Party UK, Blogging, Hardware Hacking. Will decide on the day.
