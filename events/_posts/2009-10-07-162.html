GeekUp Sheffield takes place on the first Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

  • Rocket Science - Matthew White reveals how easy it is to launch your own rocket
  • Best Things In Life … Free? - Chris Warburton cuts through the hype and myth behind free and open source software
  • Technology Paths: Chartered Members - Keith Richardson (IET) and Colin Smythe (BCS) reveal the career value of joining a professional body
  • Creative Bandwidth - Ian Wild wants you to imagine … a dedicated broadband network connecting national cultural venues. What would you do with it?

  • Shacknet: Laser Cutter Surgery
