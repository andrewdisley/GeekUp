A first GeekUp for North Staffs & Cheshire, meeting mid-way between Crewe & Stoke-on-Trent to discuss setting up a local hackspace, and for a spot of networking between local Geeks, web professionals, and the technically-curious.

A hackspace is a place where people (artists, techies, & random passers-by) can play creatively with technology - see http://nwhackspace.wordpress.com/about/ for more info.
