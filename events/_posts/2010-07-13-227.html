GeekUp Manchester takes place on the second Tuesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

MadLab is a bring your own drinks / food venue but there is also an honesty bar if you come empty handed.

Like usual Paul Robinson should be collecting the USB Drives and redistributing them for GeekUp "MixTape" Shuffle.

If you plan to attend please register at Eventbrite.
