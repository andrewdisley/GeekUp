GeekUp Leeds takes place on the third Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

For one night only! - In the season of Bah! Humbug we're shifting across town for the December GeekUpLeeds to the Brewery Tap, New Station St., for an informal gathering - no speakers - no presentations - no nonsense - just chit-chat and beer.

GeekUp Leeds now has it's very own twitter account: @GeekUpLeeds, go on follow us.
