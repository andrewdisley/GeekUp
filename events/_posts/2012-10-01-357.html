This month we welcome three speakers:

Dom Finn (@CleverFinn) presents A Peek into the World of Kinect with Windows, Dom has been playing with the Kinect SDK for Windows and wanted to share what he has found along with some ideas on possible applications and some case studies on how it is currently being used in industry.

Andrew Ellis Chadwick (@Chadders13) presents SEO being number 1, a short talk on the importance of SEO in the modern web world.

Andrew Jones (@ArthmaelGaming) presents Designing a Peer to Peer MMOG Server, a talk on the issues addressed when designing a peer to peer massively multiplayer online game server and why peer to peer can help the genre.

About Geekup Nottingham

GeekUp Nottingham takes place on the first Monday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.
