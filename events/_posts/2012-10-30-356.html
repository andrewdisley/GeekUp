For one month only GeekUp will be occurring on the last Monday - 29th October. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

European Invasion!

In a special edition of GeekUp we welcome guest speakers from creative institutes around Europe. Topics will include interactive games development for public spaces, the development of an open hardware knitting machine, community based fabrication laboratories, and the intersection of art, science and technology in digital culture.

Guest speakers include Yves Bernard from iMal (interactive Media Art Laboratory) in Brussels; Monica Cachafeiro and Gabriel Lucas from Medialab-Prado in Madrid; Susa Pop from Public Art Lab (PAL) in Berlin; and Mar Canet a new media artist, researcher and software developer from Barcelona. Don't miss this unique gathering!

DoES Liverpool provides tea & coffee, feel free to bring your own beer or if you forget someone else will share with you.

Directions, Gostins Building is at the opposite end of Hanover Street from Liverpool Central Station, near to John Lewis and the corner with Duke Street. Go up in the lifts to the fourth floor and follow the signs to DoES Liverpool.
