This month,

Dom Finn (@CleverFinn) presents Level up with Functional Programming in F#, F# is a simple and expressive programming language. Learn the basics and find out more to become an F# Lord. The talk will include a brief talk about F# and it's place in the .NET world and how F# can be applied and used with other .NET languages followed by a coding Dojo.

About Geekup Nottingham

GeekUp Nottingham takes place on the first Monday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.
