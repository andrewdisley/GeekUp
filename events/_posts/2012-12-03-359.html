!!LOOK! NEW VENUE!!

We have a new home at Mercure hotel Nottingham (just over the road from our old venue)

Dom Finn (@CleverFinn) presents Level up with Functional Programming in F#, F# is a simple and expressive programming language. Learn the basics and find out more to become an F# Lord. The talk will include a brief talk about F# and it's place in the .NET world and how F# can be applied and used with other .NET languages followed by a coding Dojo, we'll be getting hands-on with some code so bring laptops if you can! (if you can't, don't worry, you won't miss out)

We'll also be getting a welcome from our new host, James Ting of Mercure hotel Nottingham (@MercureNotts)

About Geekup Nottingham

GeekUp Nottingham takes place on the first Monday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media. Also, beer.
