We will be giving away a full license for a Jetbrains product at this month's Geekup!

This month Ed Manley (@edthink), Research Associate at the UCL Centre for Spatial Analysis, presents Simulating Urban Movement In this presentation Ed will talk about his research into the simulation of transportation flows through London, building models of individual behaviour from large datasets. Find out more about his work on his blog Urban Movements. Ed will also be running a coding dojo, challenging us to build applications from some real world transport data.

About Geekup Nottingham

GeekUp Nottingham takes place on the first Monday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media. We welcome people at all levels and from all backgrounds, if you're interested in technology, we're interested in you. Also, beer.
