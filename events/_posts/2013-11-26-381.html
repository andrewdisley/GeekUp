GeekUp Liverpool takes place on the last Tuesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

Developing Modern WebApps with AngularJS, Yeoman and Grunt

A high level look at how AngularJS helps you build single page WebApps with an overview of some of the features that make AngularJS stand apart from other frameworks along with a quick look at supporting tools like Yeoman and Grunt that can automate the complex workflows required when building modern applications.

DoES Liverpool provides tea & coffee, feel free to bring your own beer or if you forget someone else will share with you.
