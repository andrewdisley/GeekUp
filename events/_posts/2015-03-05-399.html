GeekUp Manchester is on the first Thursday of every odd month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This month we're moving to Terrace Bar in the Northern Quarter. It's not far from the old location. Food is served and you can order it to a table in our private room upstairs. Follow @mcrgeekup for any last minute updates.
