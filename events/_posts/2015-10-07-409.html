Milton Keynes GeekUp - or MK GeekUp for short - are a regular, social meet-ups series for anyone interested or professionally involved with technology, websites, design and related geekery.

MK GeekUp is organised by @AxelSegebrecht, a digital manager, and takes place at every first Wednesday at MK Social Bar in central Milton Keynes.

All events are free and it is requested that you let everybody know whether you are attending, so we can make sure a suitable space is reserved :smile:

Find us on Twitter @MKGeekUp, private message us or email hello-at-mkgeekup-dot-org
