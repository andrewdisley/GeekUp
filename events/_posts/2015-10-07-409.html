GeekUp Milton Keynes (MK GeekUp) is on the first Wednesday of every month. It's a grass-roots knowledge sharing and networking social for folks involved or interested in all forms of technology and creative media.

This is the inaugural event in October 2015 and we are meeting at the MK Social Bar, in the theatre district of central Milton Keynes. Food will be available. Be sure to follow @mkgeekup on Twitter for any last minute updates and visit our new website mkgeekup.org.
